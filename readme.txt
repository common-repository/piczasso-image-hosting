Plugin adds a submenu to wp-admin panel for easy images uploading in PicZasso's service.

== Description ==

PicZasso is the photo sharing service where you can easily upload your photos for free. Plugin adds a submenu to wp-admin panel for easy images uploading in PicZasso's service.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `piczasso-image-hosting` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Go to `Piczasso` menu in wp-admin and upload your photos.
